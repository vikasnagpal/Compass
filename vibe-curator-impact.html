Your side of the deal

Small ask, big return.

A Vibe Curator is a guest who contributes — not staff, not a volunteer. In exchange for everything above, here's all they need from you.

  • A bed in the guest dorm — not the staff dorm.
  • Meals at the property, like any guest.
  • About 15 minutes a week to plan together.
  • Your social logins and the activity calendar.
